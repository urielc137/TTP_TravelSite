    Things to do

    We offer a vast number of different things to do while you are here. Whether you've been to Japan before, or are visiting for the first time, we've got the perfect choice for you.

    • Guided Trips

      Looking for the complete experience? Take a tour with one of our experts. They'll show you secrets that you're likely to miss otherwise.

    • Photo Tour

      Want to experience the heart of Japan? Grab your camera and take a photo tour on one of our coach buses at whichever location you are in.

    • Food Trips

      If you were wondering, Japan has the best cuisine in the world. These dishes will leave you wanting to ask for more than just seconds!

    • Racing

      Got a competitive spirit? Sign up for STREET KART Tokyo Bay. Here you will be given a whole new experience to the term "Guided Tour", as you and your group will each be given a Go-Kart, and be able to race around the streets of Japan. But be warned, you will literally be placed in public traffic, so watch out for other vehicles and pedestrians.
